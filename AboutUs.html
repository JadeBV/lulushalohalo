Introduction

We pride ourselves on crafting each halo-halo with care, using only the finest ingredients sourced locally and authentically from the Philippines. From the creamy indulgence of leche flan to the crunch of finely shaved milk ice, each layer in our halo-halo is meticulously curated to create a symphony of flavors and textures that dance on your palate. Whether you're a first-time adventurer or a seasoned halo-halo enthusiast, there's always something new to discover at Lu-Lu's.

Our Mission

At Lu-Lu's Halo-Halo, our mission is to bring the authentic taste of Filipino halo-halo directly to our customers through convenient pickup services. We're dedicated to using top-quality ingredients to craft delicious halo-halo bowls that capture the essence of Filipino tradition. Our pickup location offers a warm and friendly atmosphere, ensuring a pleasant experience for every customer. We strive to make each pickup order special, providing excellent service and a taste of Filipino hospitality with every visit. Our goal is to become your go-to destination for delicious halo-halo pickup, making every moment memorable and delightful.

Our Story

While we may be new to the scene, our commitment to quality and authenticity is unwavering. That's why we've chosen to focus solely on pick-up services for now, ensuring that every bite of Lu-Lu's Halo-Halo can be enjoyed fresh and on-the-go. Whether you're craving a nostalgic taste of home or embarking on a culinary adventure, Lu-Lu's is here to delight your taste buds and transport you to the bustling streets of Silang, Cavite with every spoonful.
