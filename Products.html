Our halo-halo starts with finely shaved milk ice giving it a creamy frosty base for all the yummy stuff. What makes our halo-halo special is its mix of different textures and flavors. It has tapioca pearls, sweetened beans, jackfruit and banana for added chewiness and natural sweetness. You'll also find cubes of colorful nata de coco and gulaman, bursting with fruity flavors. And you can't forget the ube that adds a unique sweetness to every bite. We are also generous with the smooth and creamy leche flan, ensuring that there's a sufficient amount of it on top of our halo-halo and soft strips of young coconut that contrast nicely with the cold milk ice.
